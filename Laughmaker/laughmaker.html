About

Laughmaker is a short narrative platformer made over a single jam weekend for Global Game Jam 2024 at the Łódzki Game Jam 8 site in Łódź. The player controls a roller-skating clown navigating through a series of obstacles in a city, carrying balloons on physical ropes, on a bittersweet mission to bring one last smile to a child.

The game was built in Unreal Engine 5 with version control managed on a self-hosted Perforce Helix Core server — the first Jam that we used Perforce on. It was also the first one without any technical issues apart from Unreal Engine crashing!
