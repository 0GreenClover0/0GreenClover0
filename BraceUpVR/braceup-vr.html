About

BraceUp VR is a commercial VR game released on Steam and the Meta Store, developed at Virtual Software House. It blends arcade minigame action with home fitness, structuring sessions as alternating rounds of exercise and break activity — the better you perform in the exercises, the more stamina you carry into the duel. The result is a game that's genuinely competitive and actually gets your heart rate up.

The online multiplayer supports cross-platform play between Steam VR and Meta Quest players, built on Photon PUN. The solo mode offers a full offline progression, letting players challenge AI opponents across all four environments on escalating difficulty settings.

"Brace yourself — this combination guarantees you an adrenaline rush with lots of fun!"

Key Features

  • Solo offline mode with AI opponent
  • 4 environments with unique styles
  • 4 break activity / weapon modes
  • 6 exercise types for stamina
  • Easy / Medium / Hard / Hero difficulty

  • Meta Avatar support
